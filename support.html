‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my first journal entry?
Tap the "New Entry" button on the home screen. You can type your thoughts or use the voice transcription feature by tapping the microphone icon. When finished, tap "Save" to store your entry.
How does voice transcription work?
MeetMemento uses speech recognition to convert your voice into text. Grant microphone permissions when prompted, then tap and hold the microphone button while speaking. Your words will be transcribed in real-time.
What are AI-powered insights?
Our AI analyzes your journal entries to identify emotional patterns, themes, and provide personalized reflections. Insights help you understand yourself better and track your personal growth over time.
Is my journal data private and secure?
Yes! Your journal entries are encrypted and stored securely. We never share your personal data with third parties. All AI processing is done securely, and your entries remain completely private.
What's included in the free version?
The free version includes up to 10 journal entries, basic AI insights, voice transcription, and all core journaling features. It's perfect for trying out MeetMemento!
What benefits does Memento+ Premium provide?
Premium subscribers get unlimited journal entries, advanced AI insights, priority support, and access to all future premium features. Choose between monthly ($4.99) or annual ($39.99) subscriptions.
How do I restore my subscription?
Go to Settings ‚Üí Subscription, then tap "Restore Purchases". Your subscription will be automatically restored if you previously purchased it with the same Apple ID.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to iPhone Settings ‚Üí [Your Name] ‚Üí Subscriptions ‚Üí MeetMemento, then select "Cancel Subscription". You'll retain premium access until the end of your billing period.
Can I export my journal entries?
Currently, export functionality is being refined to ensure data integrity across all devices. Check back in future updates for enhanced export options.
The app isn't loading my entries. What should I do?
Try these steps: 1) Check your internet connection, 2) Close and reopen the app, 3) Ensure you're signed in with the correct account, 4) If the issue persists, contact support with details about the error message.

üîß Troubleshooting

Voice Transcription Not Working

Go to iPhone Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Security ‚Üí Microphone ‚Üí Enable MeetMemento

Sign In Issues

Ensure you're using the same sign-in method (Apple/Google/Email) you originally used. Check your internet connection and try again.

Subscription Not Activating

Wait a few moments after purchase, then restart the app. If the issue persists, tap "Restore Purchases" in Settings ‚Üí Subscription.

App Crashes or Freezes

Update to the latest version from the App Store. If the issue continues, try deleting and reinstalling the app (your cloud data is safe).
